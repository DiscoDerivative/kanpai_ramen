Kanpai Ramen

Welcome to Kanpai Ramen!

Our authentic Japanese ramen shop, where we pride ourselves on delivering an unforgettable culinary experience. Our menu showcases an array of exquisite flavors. Using only the freshest ingredients sourced locally, we create tantalizing bowls of comfort that will transport you straight to the heart of Japan. Indulge in the rich umami of our shoyu ramen, where fragrant soy sauce delicately intertwines with savory broth.

For those seeking a more robust taste, our tonkotsu ramen features a creamy pork bone broth that simmered to perfection for hours, creating a velvety texture that will leave you longing for more. The journey doesn't end with just ramen; we also offer mouthwatering desserts like our hokkaido milk cheesecake and matcha cheesecake. It pairs well with green tea which we sell in the Ito En Brand, a drink native to Japan and loved by many.
